the Rules

  • Before asking a question, do some research of your own
  • Do not derail conversations in progress
  • Use channels for their intended purposes
  • Please do not use the bot outside of #off-topic
  • No Low Effort Content, you should have spent a reasonable amount of time on something before asking for feedback
  • Spamming is not tolerated
  • No NSFW content
  • No self promotion of your works - Blatant advertising, click bait links to your own works, ect.. If you're unsure please message A moderator
  • Be respectful to every user
  • Listen to Moderators / Admins, If you have issues with a moderator, PM an admin or Toby
  • Zero tolerance policy on bullying people for any reason, please report anyone involved in this practice to a member of the moderation team. It might result in an instand kick/ban
