This months questions:

What's the story line based on and how completed is it?

Our story is based on Epistle 3. Our interpretation of it is nearly finished; we got most of that stuff sorted quite a while ago.

When will it be playable? When will there be a beta release?

We've already started supporting playtester builds which will be updated multiple times a month. We don't know when the multiplayer mode will become available, probably some time before we enter open beta on steam.

How are you planning to ensure the game doesn't enter development hell and never release like the majority of HL2 mods, especially from an inexperienced team?

We have decent team chemistry and understand how to be effective and efficient when it comes to Source Engine development. We understand how to playtest properly and have access to great advice from more experienced game developers.
